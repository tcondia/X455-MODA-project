Behind every Moda collection is a deeply collaborative force: a global team of designers whose backgrounds span continents, disciplines, and schools of thought. United by a shared philosophy of bold minimalism and expressive form, the team brings together a range of creative identities—couturiers, textile artists, architects, and visual storytellers—each shaping Moda’s evolving aesthetic from a unique point of view. Their collective experience spans Paris, Seoul, São Paulo, New York, and beyond, forming a cultural mosaic that is both the soul and signature of the brand. From the earliest sketches to the final fittings, the design team works in tight rhythm—drawing inspiration from art, movement, memory, and the built environment. While each designer contributes a distinct perspective, their collaboration fuels a singular vision: fashion that transcends trend, that speaks with precision, and that balances structure with emotion. Their process is as rigorous as it is intuitive, blending cutting-edge techniques with time-honored craftsmanship to create garments that feel both modern and eternal.

Over the years, Moda’s design team has shaped more than just collections—they’ve helped define a new language of style. Their work has appeared in leading fashion publications, on red carpets, and in galleries and museum exhibitions. More importantly, it has resonated with a new generation of wearers who seek clothing that is intelligent, expressive, and inclusive. Moda’s commitment to representation is reflected not only in who it designs for, but in who does the designing. The team includes voices across gender, culture, and lived experience—an intentional choice that brings richness and authenticity to every piece.

In a landscape often driven by ego and hierarchy, Moda’s studio culture is refreshingly horizontal. Designers are encouraged to challenge each other, to experiment, and to move beyond the constraints of category. Their ideas flow across disciplines, blurring lines between fashion and sculpture, utility and fantasy. The result is a portfolio of work that is as conceptually rigorous as it is emotionally resonant—design that doesn’t just dress the body, but invites the wearer into a narrative of form, identity, and transformation.

Above, we introduce two designers whose contributions continue to shape Moda’s voice and expand its reach. Their stories are just a glimpse into the depth of talent behind the brand—an ensemble whose collective impact is redefining what a fashion house can be in the 21st century.
